Why It Matters

Art shouldn't disappear when budgets get cut.

According to the National Center for Education Statistics, only 28% of schools offer visual arts courses meeting state standards. The Art Lab exists so students never lose access to creative education.

The gap is growing. That's why The Art Lab exists. →
